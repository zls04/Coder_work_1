Taylor Swift, born in Pennsylvania on December 13, 1989, is an American female singer, songwriter, music producer and actor. In 2006, he released his first music album Taylor Swift, which was certified by the American recording industry association and sold five times platinum records. In 2008, he released the music album fearless, which won the championship of 11 weeks on the billboard album list in the United States, certified 7 times the sales of platinum records, and won the 52nd Grammy annual album award. In 2010 , he released the music album "speak now", and the single "mean" won two Grammy Awards. In the same year, he won the hall of fame "Hal David Starlight Award". In 2011, with the sales of three physical albums, it obtained the sales certification of "five platinum records " in China. In 2012, he began to transform to a pop singer and released the music album red. The physical version of the album obtained the sales certification of "double platinum record" in China. In 2013, he won the peak award of the 47th Country Music Association Award.

Here Are Some Of Her Albums!!

1989

1989 is the fifth studio album recorded by American female singer Taylor Swift. The music director and executive producer of the album are taylor swift and Max Martin. It was released through big machine records on October 27, 2014. In December 2014, the album was rated as one of the "top ten best albums of the year" by Billboard magazine, ranking No. 1

Reputation

Reputation is the sixth studio album of American female singer Taylor Swift. Taylor Swift is the music director. Jack Antonov, Max Martin and others participated in the music production. The album contains a total of 15 songs and was released through the big machine record company on November 10, 2017. From 2018 to 2019, reputation was nominated for the Billboard Music Award "best selling album", the National Music Award "best pop rock album" and the 61st Grammy Award "best pop album"

Folklore

Folklore is the eighth studio album of American female singer Taylor Swift. The music producers of the album are taylor swift and Aaron desner. There are 16 songs in the standard album and one more in the luxury album, which was released by universal records on July 24, 2020. In July 2020, the album was awarded the certification of "female singer album with the most streaming in soundfield on the first day of release" by Guinness World Records. In March 2021, the album won the 63rd annual Grammy Award

Evermore

Evermore is the ninth studio album of American female singer Taylor Swift. The music producers of the album include Aaron dessner, Bryce dessner, etc. the standard album contains 15 songs, and the luxury album contains more than 2 songs. It was released independently on December 11, 2020. In 2020, the album was certified by the Guinness Book of world records as "the first female album in the top 200 albums of the billboard in the United States, and the shortest female album in the first time from the top 200 albums". In 2021, the included song "will" was nominated for "best pop song" and "best director" in the 38th MTV Music Video Award.
